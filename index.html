Who Are They?

Priestess

  • Game: Arknights
  • Role: NPC / Mysterious Figure
  • Origin: First Civilization Survivor
  • Connection: Partner to the Doctor

One of the last survivors of the ancient civilization. She placed the Doctor in the Chernobog Sarcophagus and appears to be the template for PRTS.

"My darling, there will be the day when we shall meet again even if the galaxies vanish into the void."

The Herta

  • Game: Honkai: Star Rail
  • Role: 5-Star Ice Erudition
  • Origin: Genius Society #83
  • Status: Emanator of Erudition

The true form behind the puppets. Co-creator of the Simulated Universe. Lives at the edge of the cosmos, a being of near-divine intellect.

"If they dared to write 'Herta Prime,' then I would call myself THE Herta. Short, simple, straight to the point, and elegant."

Lost History

⚠️ SPOILER WARNING: Contains deep lore from Arknights and Honkai: Star Rail ⚠️

The Origin of Corruption

Priestess is responsible for the "corruption" of Originium, an experiment described as "mad" by Trevor Friston. Alongside the Doctor, she created Kal'tsit (AMa-10).

The Genius of Erudition

Member #83 of the Genius Society and an Emanator of Nous. She created the Simulated Universe to study Aeons. Her true body rarely manifests.

Rhodes Island Connection

Her presence lingers in Rhodes Island through PRTS, whose personality is modeled after her. The watermark "EYESOFPRIESTESS" watches over every tactical move.

THE Herta Prime

Since Version 3.0, "The Herta" has taken the field herself, asserting her dominance over the cosmic stage, refusing the title "Prime" for simplicity.
